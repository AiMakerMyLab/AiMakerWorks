About AiMaker

We are a passionate team offering custom printing services for T-shirts, mugs, vinyl stickers, and more. Located in Pampanga, Philippines, AiMaker turns ideas into vibrant prints using sublimation, heat press, and vinyl technologies. Whether for personal use or business, we deliver high-quality, fast-turnaround products.
